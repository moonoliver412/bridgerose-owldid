Welcome, Parents

A warm welcome awaits.

Choosing a school is one of the most important decisions a family makes. At Bridgerose Jewels Academy, we make joining simple, and we are always happy to answer your questions.

Admissions are open throughout the year, subject to availability of places.

What to Bring

Admission requirements

  • A copy of the child's birth certificate
  • Recent passport-size photographs
  • Previous school report (for transfers)
  • Immunization card (Playgroup–PP2)

Requirements are indicative — please confirm the current list with the school office.

Admission Process

Five simple steps.

1

Inquire

Get in touch by phone or email, or send the inquiry form below.

2

Visit the school

Come for a tour, meet our staff and see the campus and classrooms.

3

Collect & submit the form

Complete the application form and return it with the required documents.

4

Assessment

A simple assessment or interview, where applicable, to place your child well.

5

Pay fees & join

Settle the term fees and receive your joining instructions. Welcome aboard!

When to Apply

Apply any time.

Admissions are open throughout the year, subject to the availability of places in each class. Applying early helps us reserve a space for your child.

Our terms follow the Kenyan school calendar. Please contact the office for the dates of the current and upcoming terms.
