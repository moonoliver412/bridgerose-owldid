About Bridgerose Jewels Academy.

A caring primary school in Utawala, Nairobi, where strong academics and a warm community help every child grow.

Our Story

Built on a love for learning.

Bridgerose Jewels Academy was founded to give families in Utawala a school that combines academic excellence with genuine care for each child.

From a small beginning, we have grown into a full primary school serving Playgroup through Grade 6 — while keeping the close, family feel that parents value most.

Our Mission

What we set out to do.

To nurture confident, capable and well-rounded learners through a caring, competency-based education that helps every child discover and grow their gifts.

Our Vision

Where we are heading.

To be a leading primary school in Utawala — known for brilliance, strong character and genuine care for every learner.
