Welcome

A school that feels like a second home.

Bridgerose Jewels Academy is a private primary school in Utawala, Nairobi, serving families across Embakasi East. We welcome children from Playgroup through to Grade 6.

Our teaching follows the Kenyan Competency-Based Curriculum, blending strong academics with French, Computer Studies, Music and a full range of co-curricular activities — all in a safe, caring environment where every child is known by name.
